Edit tags

  • mc:repeatable="" - repeat content
  • mc:edit="" - edit text or image
  • mc:variant="" - variants of repeatable blocks
  • mc:hideable - hide block
  • mc:allowtext - use for varant usage (show image or text)
  • mc:allowdesigner - edit images and bg

Merge tags

  • *|ARCHIVE|* - creates the URL to your campaign
  • *|DATE:|* - shows the current date
  • *|FNAME|* - inserts your subscriber's first name
  • *|LNAME|* - inserts your subscriber's last name
  • *|EMAIL|* - inserts your subscriber's email
  • *|MC:SUBJECT|* - displays the campaign's subject
